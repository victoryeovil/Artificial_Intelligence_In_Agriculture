Why Use Weather Forecasting?

Weather forecasting can provide many advantages, including:

  • Helping you prepare for extreme weather events, such as hurricanes, tornadoes, or snowstorms
  • Allowing you to plan outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, or sports events, based on the expected weather conditions
  • Assisting with agricultural planning and crop management, by predicting weather patterns and rainfall amounts
  • Enabling energy companies to optimize energy production and distribution, based on expected demand and weather conditions
  • Helping transportation companies and airlines to plan routes and schedules, based onexpected weather conditions and potential delays
